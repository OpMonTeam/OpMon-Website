Detailed Description

A trainer.

The TrainerEvent class is a basic meta event (Not a LinearMetaEvent) containing three events : two TalkingCharaEvents and one BattleEvent. The first TalkingCharaEvent represents the trainer before the battle, the BattleEvent represents the battle and the second TalkingCharaEvent represents the trainer after the battle.
